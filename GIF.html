Animated GIF

I created a GIF which would be used in a social media campaign to promote the launch of the Tropicalia travel agency. I found a vintage map JPEG on Getty Images and manipulated the colours using Image - Adjustments - Colour Balance to give it a sepia hue. I also sourced a JPEG of a map pin and cut this out using the quick selection tool. I created 24 individual jpeg images by overlaying the letters, flowers, and map pins in the order I wanted them to appear. I then opened these as a stack in photoshop. I then opened the timelines and created a frame animation, creating frames from the layered jpegs I played around with the timing of each frame and decided I wanted the writing to appear quickly , to give the impression of the logo being typed, and then for the GIF to pause on the completed logo and completed map. I saved for web as 128 with no dither
